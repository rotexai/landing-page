# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Vietnamese founders and business operators who repeatedly run the same operational processes and want to reduce cost without handing control to an opaque AI agent. Technical workflow builders are a secondary audience who need to inspect, test, and maintain the generated automation.

## Product Purpose

RotexAI turns a natural-language description of recurring work into a transparent, reusable workflow and a purpose-built web interface. AI helps create and change the process; validated deterministic automation handles routine runs so teams do not pay an AI model to rethink the same operation every time.

## Brand Personality

Pragmatic, transparent, assured.

## Anti-references

Do not present RotexAI as a generic ERP, a magical black-box AI agent, a playful no-code toy, or a developer-only workflow canvas. Avoid unsupported savings claims and vague “AI does everything” language.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with the operational outcome, then reveal the underlying mechanism.
2. Make the build-once, run-repeatedly economic model concrete.
3. Show that every generated workflow remains inspectable and controllable.
4. Present custom interfaces as the bridge between technical automation and everyday work.
5. Prefer specific process examples over broad AI promises.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA with keyboard navigation, visible focus, sufficient contrast, semantic headings, reduced-motion support, and Vietnamese-first copy that avoids unnecessary technical jargon.
